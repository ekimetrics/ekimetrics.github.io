## Plenary Speakers

### [Sébastien Destercke](https://www.hds.utc.fr/~sdesterc/dokuwiki/), CNRS Senior Researcher, CID team leader

**Abstract**: Mini-course on uncertainty in statistics, covering key concepts and methods in the field. 

### [Yedidia AGNIMO](https://www.linkedin.com/in/yedidia-agnimo-b90131161/?originalSubdomain=fr), Doctoral Researcher at Ekimetrics and Inria

**Abstract**: Disucssion of uncertainty quantification and hallucination detection in large language models. 

### [Gianni Franchi](https://giannifranchi.github.io/), Research Director at AMIAD leading the Trustworthy AI group

**Abstract**: Presentation of Torch-uncertainty, a deep learning framework for uncertainty quantification.

### [Evaggelia Pitoura](https://www.cs.uoi.gr/~pitoura/), Lead Researcher at Archimedes Research Unit, ATHENA
